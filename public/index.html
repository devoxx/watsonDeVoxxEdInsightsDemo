This service uses a pre-existing graph of concepts (based on Wikipedia) to surface the concepts in your input. It will use these concepts to recommend relevant content from the corpus you ingest. The Alchemy API is also used to analyze text through natural language processing and help us to understand its sentiment and emotions.

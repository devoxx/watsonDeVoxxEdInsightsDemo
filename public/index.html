Watson DeVoxxEd

Here you can discover Voxxed and Devoxx related content which may or may not be found using a traditional keyword search.

The Watson DeVoxxEd Project

This service uses a pre-existing graph of concepts (based on Wikipedia) to surface the Voxxed and Devoxx concepts. It will use these concepts to recommend relevant content from our corpus. The Alchemy API is also used to analyze text through natural language processing and helps us to understand its sentiment and emotions. And the Speech to Text service has been used to create transcripts of all the Devoxx talks!
